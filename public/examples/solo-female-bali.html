üõ°Ô∏è Solo Female Safety Tips

  • Bali is generally very safe for solo female travelers
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples - sarong and sash required
  • Use reputable transport apps like Gojek or Grab
  • Avoid isolated beaches and rice fields after dark
  • Be cautious of drink spiking in tourist bars - watch your drinks
  • Keep copies of passport and visa separate from originals
  • Learn basic Indonesian phrases for emergencies
  • Trust local women's advice - they understand the culture best

üèõÔ∏è Cultural Respect Guide

Temple Etiquette:

  • Clothing: Sarong and sash required (can rent at temples)
  • Menstruation: Traditional belief prohibits temple entry during menstruation
  • Behavior: Remove shoes, don't point feet toward shrines
  • Photography: Ask permission before photographing people
  • Offerings: Don't step on flower offerings on ground
  • Respect: Lower your head when passing in front of people praying
