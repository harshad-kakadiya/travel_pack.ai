‚ö†Ô∏è Extreme Altitude Safety

  • HACE/HAPE Recognition: Know cerebral/pulmonary edema symptoms
  • Immediate Descent: Only treatment for severe altitude sickness
  • Weather Monitoring: Himalayan weather kills - respect forecasts
  • Communication: Satellite phone/GPS beacon mandatory
  • Evacuation Insurance: Helicopter rescue costs $10,000+
  • Buddy System: Never travel alone above 14,000 ft
  • Turnaround Times: Set strict time limits regardless of summit desire
  • Emergency Shelter: Know how to build snow caves
